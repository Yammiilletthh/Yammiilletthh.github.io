Fufu

Fufu is a beautiful Russian blues, with their smooth blue/grey fur. She was born outside. The residents had found homes for the kittens from prior litters, but this time there were no more homes for these babies.

Fufu has a bigger white spot in her chest. While Fufu will sit for purrs and cuddles, she immediately perks up for play. She absolutely loves the wand toys and has recently discovered catnip toys are pretty fun too. She in a foster home with other cats and two dogs. She would love to go to a new home with a sibling or other kitten around the same age for double fun.
